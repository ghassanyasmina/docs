## Introduction
You can use Yasmina Embedded Price Comparison to fetch and display offers from various insurance companies.
These Embedded Price Comparison are connected to multiple insurance products and can retrieve different package options along with their pricing and benefits.

Yasmina has different aggregators for different insurance. Currently we are supporting Motor and SME Medical. To use any aggregator
you will need to get an OAuth 2.0 token
